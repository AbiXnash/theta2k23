About Robotics

The cluster aims to provide a platform for like-minded individuals to come together and learn about robotics, programming, electronics, and mechanics. Members can collaborate on various projects, from building basic robots to machines, using advanced autonomous a range of tools and technologies. The cluster may also participate in robotics competitions, hackathons, and other events to test their skills and showcase their creations.
