About our Events

Capture the flag?

A Capture the Flag (CTF) event is a cybersecurity competition where participants try to solve a series of challenges that involve hacking, cryptography, forensics, and other skills related to cybersecurity.

Code a thon?

Software Development Competition that challenges participants to develop a project or solution in a limited amount of time to a given scenario.
The best 3 Solutions will be Shortlisted and provided with prizes.

Tech Quiz?

Join our Tech Quiz competition and put your tech knowledge to the test! The event is open to individuals and teams of up to four members, with separate tracks for students and professionals. Get ready to compete, have fun, and win exciting prizes!

Ideathon?

Anyone with a tech-based idea is eligible to participate in the event, which exclusively welcomes project ideas related to technology. It's a chance to showcase your passion and creativity in the field of technology and receive recognition for your innovative ideas. Join in and share your ideas with others who share the same passion for technology!
